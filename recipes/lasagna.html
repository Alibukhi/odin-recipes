Lasagna

A yummy and delicious lasagna to fulfill your meat and cheese craving!

  • Ingredients:
      Meat (of choice)
      Cheeses
      Onion and Garlic
      Tomato products
      Spices and Seasoning
      Lasagna Noodles
      Eggs
      Sugar
  • Steps:
      Make the Meat Sauce
      Cook the Noodles
      Make the Ricotta mixture
      Layer the Lasagna
      Cover with foil and Bake
      Let the Lasagna rest before serving
